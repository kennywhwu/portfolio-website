Hi there, I'm Kenny Hwu
and here's a little about me...

I'm a highly inquisitive and curious student of the world, always interested in tinkering and ceaselessly wondering how everything works. My pursuit of web development and software engineering has become a natural extension of these qualities, and I'm excited and dedicated to building the best products through these practices.

I took a chance on my education with the unlikely combination of Finance and Engineering degrees at the University of Pennsylvania. Through them, I ultimately discovered a sweetspot between numbers-centered analytical problem-solving, and optimization-based intuitive business strategy. I've internalized rapid personal growth working on customer behavior management at Capital One, marketing and retail analytics at Oportun, and finally new product and growth initiatives at LendingClub. These experiences have enabled me to explore a wide variety of roles within the financial services and fintech industry, as well as the increasing significance that technology commands in the marketplace. I've devoted myself to diving headfirst into that world of technology, stepping into my next chapter, and thriving in a software development career!
