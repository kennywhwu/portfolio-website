Devolve

Asymmetric multiplayer game played over websockets


Play* Code

Play Together

Devolve is a React-based game that is intended to play over multiple online devices (desktop/mobile). Players that access the website play a dynamic asymmetric game, where one player is larger and chases the other smaller players who are trying to escape. Multiplayer is achieved by an Express backend that connects to clients through websockets.

*Play is intended for multiple players. For demo purposes, please open multiple tabs to simulate multiple players

Lobby

When players access the website, they are queued up in a lobby waiting room. Each player can mark or unmark themselves ready. Once all players are ready, the game can begin


Gameplay

Once the game starts, all players are thrown onto a bounded board, the size of which is proportional to the number of players. One player is "the Beast," a large, red, monstrosity that thinks only of chasing down and consuming the helpless prey. As time goes by, the Beast grows at a rate dependent on number of players. The remaining smaller players run for their lives through a continuous randomly-generated exit. The game is over when all smaller players have either escaped or been consumed.
